WHAT IS QUANTOM?

THE FAST FOOD
MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

QUANTOM (Quantity + Order + Management) is an End-to-End (E2E) operational model that focuses on fast service, high efficiency, and consistent food quality. It uses standardized processes, quick preparation methods, and digital systems such as POS to manage orders and operations.

Ordering Made Easy!

Customers can place orders quickly as a Guest. Want to unlock special promos? Encourage them to log in and turn casual buyers into loyal members!

Fast Kitchen, Happy Customers!

Orders are instantly sent to the right outlet and crew. Order status is clearly tracked from preparation to ready to serve. Efficient and transparent!

Automatic Restocking

The system alerts you when stock is running low. Record supplier purchases and access restock history in just a few clicks. Never run out!
